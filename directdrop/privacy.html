DirectDrop

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

DirectDrop transfers files between your Android device and a browser on the same Wi-Fi network. All transfers happen locally — no data ever leaves your network or reaches our servers.

1. What DirectDrop Does

DirectDrop runs a temporary HTTP server on your device (port 8080) that allows a browser on the same local Wi-Fi network to download or upload files. The connection is established using a QR code or a local IP address displayed on screen. No internet connection is required or used for file transfers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • The files you transfer
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

DirectDrop stores the following preferences in your device's local app storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Display theme (dark or light mode)
  • Language selection
  • UI panel states (e.g. whether the "How it works" section is expanded)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

4. Local Network Usage

When you share or receive files, your device's local IP address is shown on screen so another device on the same network can connect. The IP addresses of connecting devices are briefly displayed in the app UI for your information but are not logged, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

The file transfer server shuts down automatically when you stop sharing or close the app.

5. File Access

DirectDrop requests access to your files solely to let you select files you wish to share. We do not scan, index, or access any files other than those you explicitly choose to transfer.

6. Third-Party Services

DirectDrop does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, or crash-reporting services that transmit data externally. The app is distributed through:

  • Google Play Store — subject to Google's own privacy policy

7. Children's Privacy

DirectDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@spotrobotics.app
