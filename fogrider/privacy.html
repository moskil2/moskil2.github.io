FogRider

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

FogRider runs entirely in your browser. GPX tracks you load and profiles you save are stored locally on your device — nothing is ever uploaded to our servers.

1. What FogRider Does

FogRider is a browser-based GPX track viewer and analyzer. It lets you load GPX files, visualize routes on an interactive map, and analyze the speed, frequency, and surface type of your tracks.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

FogRider stores the following data in your browser's local storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • GPX tracks you load and their colors/display settings
  • Saved "profiles" (named collections of tracks you choose to save for later)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for spotrobotics.app, or by not saving a profile.

3. Third-Party Services FogRider Connects To

FogRider has no backend server of its own. When certain features are used, your browser connects directly to the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — used to fetch tourist points of interest and to classify the surface type of your tracks (asphalt, gravel, forest path, etc.). Only geographic coordinates are sent; no personal data is included.
  • Komoot API — if you choose to import activities from Komoot, your browser connects directly to Komoot's API using the login credentials you provide. Your credentials and session token are used only within your browser session and are never sent to or stored on our servers.
  • Map tiles — base map imagery is loaded from OpenStreetMap and CARTO, subject to their own privacy practices.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

Because FogRider has no backend server, we do not collect, log, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your GPX tracks or location data
  • Your Komoot login credentials
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports

5. Children's Privacy

FogRider is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
